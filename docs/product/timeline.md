# Timeline Intelligence

See `docs/product/timeline-events.md` for the base aquarium timeline model.

Aquarium Intelligence builds deterministic timeline insights from normalized aquarium-linked records: timeline events, readings, water changes, livestock changes, losses, conditions, maintenance, equipment changes, workflows, breeding context, and emergency context.

Insights answer review questions such as what changed before a condition or loss, whether similar records appeared before, and what evidence is missing. They use cautious temporal language and never claim causation from proximity alone.
